Behind and Beyond the Film

An insight into the lives of the New York University Abu Dhabi students as they flood into the library everyday in pursuit of knowledge. They know no limit to this journey, they will spend all day and night in any space that will provide them with the information they seek.

The film highlights this environment that encourages students to work hard and pursue their unwavering goals without any distractions. The music accompanying the film portrays the intensity of this journey as it is without a doubt not an easy one.

The main character of the film is a bioengineering student in NYU Abu Dhabi where the film takes place and she is one of those dedicated students in pursuit of knowledge. The film follows her through one of her studying sessions where she studies and researches concepts throughout the film.

The film not only hopes to capture the journey of such students but inspire others to follow in their footsteps and work towards achieving their goals and pursuing education. In a way, it is a motivational film that hopes to evoke the feeling of hope within students.
